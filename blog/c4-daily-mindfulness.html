Daily Mindfulness

C4: 23rd Jan 2016

Search Inside Yourself: Chapter 3

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

It felt a bit awkward in a controlled environment to sustain a monologue as often we felt the urge to fill the silence. It was a nice to have that dedicated time to be listened to and it was a helpful exercise in ensuring understanding and efficient listening/memory retention.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

Most likely the ‘do less than you can’ and the ‘one breath a day techniques’. Start small and build from that.

Any other musings?

I liked the idea of joyful mindfulness and enjoying more out of the moment and the analogy of handling listening, looping, and dipping with having peripheral vision.
