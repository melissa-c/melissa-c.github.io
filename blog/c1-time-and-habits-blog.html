Time and Habit Techniques

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

As I've only just read about the different techniques, I haven't tried many yet. I did the timebox technique of an hour and that was a good amount of time to focus without getting out of a rhythm. I also try to get up, do stretches and clear my mind for a few minutes regularly. But I can get caught up in being in "the zone" and forget to take more regular breaks.

What did you find interesting?

The tiny habits and creating habits techniques sounded realistic, logical, and achieveable. So I will definitely try the chocolate reward system ;)

What worked for you?

Getting up regularly works for me. Though sometimes I lose track of time so I will incorporate some of the Pomodoro technique by setting a timer to be more strict with the time and hopefully strengthen the time it takes to focus.

Which will you experiment further with?

I will experiment more with the tiny habits and timeboxing. They seem to be a good fit for my working/learning style.
