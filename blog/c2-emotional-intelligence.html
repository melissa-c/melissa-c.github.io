Emotional Intelligence

C2: 21st Dec 2015

Search Inside Yourself: Chapter 1

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

I found the idea of having space/time between the stimulus and the response - and to consciously exercise this mindful state in order to strengthen and hone it - was insightful and helpful. I appreciated the scientific perspective and approach to emotional intelligence and mindfulness as I tend to be quite skeptical of people who I think are spouting woo woo.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

It was peaceful and calming. My mind did wander occasionally and often external noises were distracting but I just tried to let those things go and focus on my breathing. Afterwards my heart rate seemed slower than when I started.
